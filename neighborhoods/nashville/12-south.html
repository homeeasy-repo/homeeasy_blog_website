Getting Around

12 South's commercial strip is walkable -- restaurants, shops, and Sevier Park are all on foot. Beyond the strip, the neighborhood becomes residential and car-dependent. Downtown is a 10-15 minute drive. The Gulch is about the same. WeGo bus service runs along 12th Avenue but frequency is lower than the core routes. A car is useful here for anything outside the immediate strip. Biking is an option -- the terrain is relatively flat between 12 South and The Gulch.
