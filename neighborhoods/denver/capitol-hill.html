The Trade-Offs

What You Get

Most walkable neighborhood in Denver. 118 buildings -- the deepest selection in any single Denver neighborhood. Broadway nightlife. Colfax Avenue culture. LGBTQ+ community hub. Cheesman Park and Denver Botanic Gardens. 10-15 minute walk to downtown. Victorian architecture mixed with modern construction. The Fillmore and Ogden Theatre for live music.

What You Give Up

Parking is difficult in the dense residential blocks. Some blocks along Colfax can feel rough, especially late at night. Per-square-foot pricing is mid-range -- not the cheapest in the metro, but not the most expensive either. Concession data is still thin for this neighborhood.

Getting Around

Walk Score around 90. Transit Score around 65. Broadway, Colfax Avenue, and 17th Avenue provide dense commercial corridors. RTD bus routes run along all major corridors -- the 0 and 15 on Colfax are among the most frequent in Denver's system. Downtown is a 10-15 minute walk west. Bike lanes on several streets with B-cycle stations throughout. A car is useful but not necessary.
