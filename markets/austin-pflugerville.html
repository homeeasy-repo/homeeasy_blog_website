Why Pflugerville?

Pflugerville (pronounced "FLOO-ger-vill") has become one of the most popular destinations for renters who want Austin-area living without Austin-area pricing. Sitting just northeast of the city along IH-35 and SH-130, Pflugerville offers a diverse, family-oriented community with a Deutschen Pfest celebration that nods to its German heritage. Lake Pflugerville provides kayaking, fishing, and a popular 3-mile trail loop, and the city's park system has expanded rapidly to serve the growing population.

Pflugerville ISD serves the city and consistently performs above state averages on academic metrics. The tech commute is what draws many renters here: the Domain, Apple's Austin campus, Dell, Samsung's fab in nearby Taylor, and the growing Tesla Gigafactory are all within a 20 to 35 minute drive depending on direction. The SH-130 toll road bypasses Austin traffic entirely for commuters heading to Samsung, Tesla, or Dell in Round Rock. Stone Hill Town Center provides local shopping and dining, reducing the need to drive into Austin for everyday errands.

Average one-bedroom rents in Pflugerville run about $1,300, which represents significant savings compared to apartments inside Austin city limits. Newer communities along SH-130 and Wells Branch Parkway offer modern amenities and list in the $1,400 to $1,600 range.
