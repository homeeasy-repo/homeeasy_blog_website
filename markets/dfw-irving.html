Why Irving?

Irving's Las Colinas district is one of DFW's premier business addresses, home to major corporate campuses including ExxonMobil's former headquarters campus, Kimberly-Clark, and numerous Fortune 500 companies. The area combines a polished urban feel with lakeside living around Lake Carolyn, where you can walk or kayak steps from your apartment. DART's Orange Line provides direct rail access to DFW Airport and downtown Dallas, making Irving one of the best-connected suburbs in the Metroplex.

The Toyota Music Factory has transformed Irving's entertainment scene with a concert venue, restaurants, and nightlife that rival anything in Dallas proper. At an average one-bedroom rent of $1,300 per month, Irving delivers a compelling value proposition: corporate-adjacent living with genuine entertainment options at prices well below Uptown or the Design District. The city's central location between Dallas and Fort Worth means you are never more than 20 minutes from either downtown.

Irving's apartment stock ranges from luxury high-rises in Las Colinas to more affordable communities in south Irving, creating a wide pricing spectrum. Our AI analyzes these differences to find where you get the most value relative to the amenities and location that matter to you, whether that is a walkable commute to your office or quick access to DFW Airport for frequent travelers.
