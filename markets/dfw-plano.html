Why Plano?

Plano sits at the heart of DFW's tech and corporate corridor. Toyota's North American headquarters, JPMorgan Chase's major campus, and Frito-Lay's global headquarters all call Plano home, creating a dense concentration of high-paying jobs within a short commute of thousands of apartment communities. The Legacy West development has transformed the area into a walkable mixed-use destination with restaurants, shops, and luxury apartments all in one place.

Plano consistently ranks among the best places to live in Texas thanks to its top-rated Plano ISD schools, low crime rates, and extensive parks system. The city offers a suburban feel with urban amenities, and the average one-bedroom apartment runs about $1,400 per month. That price point gets you significantly more space and newer finishes than what you would find in Uptown Dallas, with a commute that many corporate employees find shorter since they are already working in the northern suburbs.

New construction in Plano continues along the Dallas North Tollway corridor, and these communities frequently offer move-in specials to fill units ahead of schedule. Our AI tracks these concessions alongside base rent to calculate your true monthly cost, helping you identify apartments where the effective rent is hundreds below the listed price.
