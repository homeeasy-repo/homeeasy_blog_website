Why Oak Brook?

Oak Brook is one of DuPage County's most prestigious addresses. The village is home to Oakbrook Center, one of the largest open-air shopping malls in the country, anchoring a corridor of high-end retail and dining. McDonald's relocated its global headquarters from Oak Brook to Chicago's West Loop in 2018, but the area remains a major corporate hub — Ace Hardware, Inland Real Estate, and TreeHouse Foods all maintain operations here. The intersection of I-88 and I-294 makes Oak Brook one of the best-connected suburbs in the western corridor.

Apartment communities in Oak Brook tend toward the premium end: newer construction with resort-style amenities, fitness centers, and covered parking. One-bedroom rents average around $1,800, with luxury units near Oakbrook Center commanding $2,200+. However, our AI regularly identifies pricing gaps — lease-up concessions on new construction and seasonal pricing dips in winter months can bring effective rents significantly below advertised rates.

The village offers excellent schools in Hinsdale Township District 86, extensive park districts with golf courses, and proximity to Morton Arboretum. Commuting to downtown Chicago takes about 40 minutes via I-88 or Metra's BNSF line from nearby Westmont or Clarendon Hills stations. For professionals working in the western suburbs, Oak Brook puts you at the center of the action without the Loop commute.
