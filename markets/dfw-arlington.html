Why Arlington?

Arlington sits right between Dallas and Fort Worth, making it the geographic center of the Metroplex and one of its most affordable rental markets. AT&T Stadium, home of the Dallas Cowboys, and Globe Life Field, home of the Texas Rangers, anchor an entertainment district that draws millions of visitors annually. Six Flags Over Texas adds to the mix, making Arlington a destination city within DFW.

The University of Texas at Arlington, one of the largest universities in the state, brings a youthful energy to the city and creates consistent demand for affordable housing. At an average one-bedroom rent of $1,100 per month, Arlington offers some of the best value in the entire Metroplex. You can find spacious apartments with modern amenities for what a studio costs in Uptown Dallas, and the I-30 and I-20 corridors provide easy access to employment centers in both Dallas and Fort Worth.

Arlington's entertainment district is undergoing significant redevelopment with new mixed-use projects, and the surrounding residential areas are seeing an influx of updated apartment communities competing for tenants. Our AI tracks these competitive dynamics to identify where oversupply is creating pricing opportunities that benefit renters looking for maximum value.
