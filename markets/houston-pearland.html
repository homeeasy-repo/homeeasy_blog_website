Why Pearland?

Pearland has been one of the fastest-growing cities in Texas for over a decade, and for good reason. Sitting just south of Houston along SH-288, it offers an easy 20-minute commute to the Texas Medical Center, the largest medical complex in the world. Healthcare professionals, nurses, and medical students make up a significant portion of Pearland's renter population, drawn by the combination of affordable rents and a short drive to work. The Pearland Town Center anchors the city's retail scene, and the area's restaurant options have expanded rapidly to serve the growing population.

Pearland ISD and Alvin ISD both serve the city, with several schools earning top ratings. The community is also popular with families working at NASA's Johnson Space Center in Clear Lake, which sits about 20 minutes to the southeast. Shadow Creek Ranch, one of the city's newer master-planned developments, features newer apartment communities with resort-style amenities at price points well below what you would pay in the Inner Loop. Average one-bedroom rents in Pearland run about $1,250, with newer construction in the $1,400 to $1,600 range and older communities near the original downtown dipping below $1,100.
