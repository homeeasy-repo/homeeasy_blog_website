Why Live Oak?

Live Oak is a small city in northeast San Antonio that punches above its weight in value for renters. Sitting along the I-35 corridor between Loop 410 and Loop 1604, Live Oak offers some of the most affordable rents in the San Antonio metro while maintaining easy access to both downtown San Antonio and the northeastern suburbs. Joint Base San Antonio-Randolph (Randolph AFB) is just minutes east, making Live Oak one of the most popular locations for military personnel and civilian defense contractors. The Forum at Olympia Parkway shopping center, anchored by Target and a variety of restaurants, provides convenient retail within the city.

Judson ISD and North East ISD both serve portions of Live Oak, and the city benefits from the broader northeast San Antonio school network that includes several highly rated campuses. Live Oak's location along I-35 makes it a natural commuter location for workers at Randolph AFB, Fort Sam Houston, and the growing medical corridor near UT Health San Antonio. The city has also attracted retail and commercial development along Pat Booker Road, reducing the need to drive elsewhere for everyday needs. Live Oak Park and the community pool give families outdoor recreation options within city limits.

Average one-bedroom rents in Live Oak hover around $1,100, making it one of the most affordable options in the San Antonio metro. Even newer communities in the area tend to list below $1,300.
