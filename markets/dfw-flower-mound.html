Why Flower Mound?

Flower Mound sits along the shores of Grapevine Lake, offering a lakeside lifestyle that is rare in the DFW Metroplex. The town has deliberately maintained a family-oriented, nature-focused identity even as it has grown to over 80,000 residents. Extensive trail systems, parks, and proximity to the lake make outdoor recreation a daily convenience rather than a weekend excursion. The Lewisville Independent School District schools in Flower Mound are among the top-rated in DFW, making this one of the most sought-after addresses for families with children.

Despite its suburban, family-oriented character, Flower Mound provides convenient access to the employment centers of both Dallas and Fort Worth via FM 2499, I-35W, and the Sam Rayburn Tollway. The Lakeside DFW development has brought mixed-use retail and dining to the community, reducing the need to travel to neighboring cities for shopping and entertainment. At an average one-bedroom rent of $1,450 per month, Flower Mound positions itself as a premium suburban choice for renters who prioritize schools, safety, and natural beauty.

Flower Mound's rental inventory is more limited than larger DFW suburbs, which means the best deals go quickly.
