Why Frisco?

Frisco has been one of the fastest-growing cities in America for the past decade, transforming from a small North Texas town into a thriving suburb with over 220,000 residents. The $5 Billion Mile along the Dallas North Tollway is home to the Dallas Cowboys' headquarters at The Star, the PGA of America, and numerous corporate offices. This concentration of development has created a self-contained employment and entertainment hub that reduces the need to commute into Dallas proper.

Young families flock to Frisco for its nationally ranked Frisco ISD schools, extensive youth sports programs, and safe neighborhoods. The rental market reflects the city's demographic with a mix of luxury apartment communities and family-friendly complexes with amenities like pools, dog parks, and playgrounds. The average one-bedroom runs about $1,500 per month, which is competitive for the quality of construction and community amenities you get in return.

Because Frisco is still growing rapidly, new apartment communities are constantly coming online. Developers competing for move-ins offer concessions that can knock hundreds off your effective monthly rent.
