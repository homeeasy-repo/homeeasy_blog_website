Why Schertz?

Schertz is a rapidly growing city northeast of San Antonio, positioned along I-35 with easy access to Joint Base San Antonio's Randolph and Fort Sam Houston installations. The military presence creates a stable economic base and a community culture that values family, service, and neighborhood connection. Schertz-Cibolo-Universal City ISD schools perform well, and the city's parks, recreation center, and community events provide a quality of life that has attracted families from across the San Antonio metro.

At an average one-bedroom rent of $1,050 per month, Schertz is one of the most affordable quality suburbs in the San Antonio area. The I-35 corridor provides access to both San Antonio and Austin, making Schertz practical for anyone working along that route. The city has invested in retail development along FM 3009, reducing the need to drive into San Antonio for everyday shopping and dining. Amazon's fulfillment center in the area has added non-military employment, diversifying the local job market.

Schertz's apartment inventory has expanded with new construction along the I-35 and FM 3009 corridors, and these communities compete for residents with attractive introductory pricing.
