About HomeEasy

HomeEasy is a technology-enabled real estate services firm. Since 2017, our team — a mix of AI agents, virtual coordinators, and field agents who physically tour buildings — has built a proprietary database of 85,000+ buildings across 8 markets. Not listing data. Intelligence gathered from thousands of in-person tours and tens of thousands of direct calls: approval policies, showing procedures, leasing contacts, and pricing history.
